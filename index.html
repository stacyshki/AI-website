Artificial Intelligence

Definition

Artificial Intelligence (AI) is intelligence demonstrated by machines, as opposed to the natural intelligence displayed by humans or animals.

Examples

AI examples include advanced web search engines, recommendation systems (used by YouTube, Amazon or Netflix), human speech recognition systems (such as Siri or Alexa), self-driving cars (e.g. Tesla), and competing at the highest level in strategic game systems (such as chess and Go).
